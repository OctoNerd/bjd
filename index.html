Who is Blackjack Davey?

Black Jack Davey is a folk rock troubadour on a never ending journey to preserve love and truth in the human experience. He's come from far and wide to your town to exploit the tycoons of special interest, rally and entertain the common folk, and bring peace and understanding to everyone.
